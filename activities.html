Activities Guide

Tilden Regional Park
Type: Park Main Entrance: Kensington, Berkeley, and Richmond Yelp Review: 4.8 stars
Things to do: Steam Train, Merry Go Round, Animal Farm, and a Lake. Price: Most are free, but varies. Website: Tilden Regional Park

Fisherman's Wharf
Type: Pier Location: Pier 39, San Francisco Yelp Review: 4 stars
Things to Do: Visit unique stores, Mirror Maze, Merry Go Round, Watch Talent Shows, & Much More! Parking: Yes but for a fee. Website: Fisherman's Wharf

San Francisco Cable Car System
Type: Metro System Location: San Francisco Yelp Review: 3.5 stars
Things To Do: Stop by unique streets like Height St. & Lombard St. Price: $8 Website: San Francisco Cable Car System

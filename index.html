Who We Are

BayLivin is a website created as a project for a Web Tech course at Babson College.

This website serves as a insider guide to all the amazing spots in the Bay Area. Most of these spots are not well known unless you are native to the Bay Area. We've got you covered from places to eat, to go out to, or even just to soak up the beautiful California sun! We'll make sure you get the most out of your experience!
